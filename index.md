# YummyBait

YummyBait is a DeFi analytics platform for Uniswap V3 and V4 — real-time
indexing, position tracking, AI-driven risk scoring, a Telegram bot for
LPs, and (coming soon) webhook signals you can route to your own agents.

This site documents the **public surface**:
